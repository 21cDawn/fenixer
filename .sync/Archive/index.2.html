Hardware Layer #1 of Fenix's Awesomeness

Fenix is fully reprogrammable, start to finish. When you buy it, you get to choose what software it runs. If you don't like that software anymore, you can always choose to put different software on fenix. No more needing to "jailbreak", "unlock", or otherwise open up your device beforehand. Of course you get a root account, Fenix is yours!
