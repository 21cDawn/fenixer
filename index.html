What is Fenix?

Fenix is a mini-pc or tv-stick that's open-source, hackable, and comes flashed with your choice of ROM. Android ROMs bring apps to your display, while Linux ROMs give total control to craft a custom device.

Fenix can

- Be an android tv-stick

- Be a portable linux workstation

- Become your pocket stick-of-hacks

- Host servers for web apps or games

- Stream your music or videos

- Download torrents

- Run alternate ROMs such as Cyanogenmod and Firefox OS

Hardware is Fenix's buttery crust layer

Fenix is fully reprogrammable, start to finish. When you buy it, you get to choose what software it runs. If you don't like that software anymore, you can always choose to put different software on fenix. No more needing to "jailbreak", "unlock", or otherwise open up your device beforehand. Of course you get your root account, Fenix is yours!
